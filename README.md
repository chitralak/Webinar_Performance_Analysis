# Webinar_Performance_Analysis
Analysis of webinar registrations, attendance, and participant feedback

**Event Overview :**
This analysis evaluates the performance of two webinars conducted by Modo Edulabs by comparing registrations, attendance, participant feedback, and engagement outcomes. The objective is to identify trends, assess improvements between events, and recommend actions to enhance future webinar effectiveness.

**Dataset Summary:**
Total events analyzed: 2
Total records after cleaning: 20
Duplicate records were removed using event_id and attendee_email to ensure accurate participant counts.

**Key fields used :**
    Attendance Status,
    Event Name,
    Satisfaction Rating,
    Recommendation Feedback and 
    Participant Comments.
    
**Key Metrics :**

 **Event 1:** Cyber Security - 1
  Total Registered : 9
  Attended: 5
  Attendance Rate : 56%
  Average Satisfaction Score (out of 10) : 6
  Recommendation Rate : 40%
  
  **Event 2:** Cyber Security - 2
  Total Registered : 11
  Attended: 9
  Attendance Rate : 82%
  Average Satisfaction Score (out of 10) : 7.5
  Recommendation Rate : 83.33%
  
**Key Insights:**

**Insight 1**: Growth in Registrations
Registrations increased from 9 to 11 participants (22.2%) , indicating growing interest in the webinar topic.

**Insight 2:** Improved Attendance Conversion
Attendance increased from 5 of 9 (56%) in Event 1 to 9 of 11 (82%) in Event 2, showing better conversion from registration to actual participation.

**Insight 3:** Engagement and Satisfaction Improved
The second event recorded higher satisfaction and recommendation rates, suggesting improvements in content delivery or session relevance.

**Insight 4:** Feedback Response Gaps
Only 66.7% of attendees provided feedback, indicating an opportunity to improve post-event response collection.

**Recommendations :**

**Increase Attendance Rate:**
    Send reminder notifications closer to the event start time.
    Clearly communicate session value and learning outcomes.
    
**Improve Engagement Quality:**
    Continue the content format used in the second event.
    Include interactive elements earlier in the session.
    
**Improve Feedback Collection:**
    Simplify feedback forms.
    Prompt attendees immediately after the session ends.
  
**Conclusion :** 

The second webinar demonstrated measurable improvement in registrations, attendance, and participant satisfaction. By applying consistent engagement strategies and improving feedback collection, future webinars can further enhance participation and overall effectiveness.
